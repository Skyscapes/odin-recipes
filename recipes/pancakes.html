Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Heat griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Mix together dry ingredients and add in wet inredients. Stir until all ingredients are wet.
  2. Place tsp of butter onto griddle or skillet. Ladle batter onto griddle or skillet. Flip after bubbles rise to surface and bottoms brown, after 2-4 minutes.
  3. Cook until second side is lightly browned.
