Pizza

Ingredients

  • 12 in round pizza dough
  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 oz fresh mozzarella
  • 4-5 roughly torn basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven with pizza stone or tiles
  2. Spread sauce on dough with back of a spoon and leave 1/2 inch edges
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the top. Break cheese into large pieces and place ontop of sauce. Scatter basil leaves over top.
  4. Pick up pie with pizza peel and slide onto heated stoe in oven. Bake until crust is golden brown, approx 4-8 minutes.
