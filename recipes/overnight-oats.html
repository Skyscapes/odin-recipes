Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old-fashiond oats
  • 1/4 cup dried fruit
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Mix oats, dried fruit, seeds, milk, and salt in a jar. Seal tightly and refrigerate for 5hrs up to 5 days.
  2. Uncover, stir, add sweetness if needed.
